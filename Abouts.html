About Us


Welcome to Royal Orchid & Regenta Hotels, a dynamic and expanding hospitality brand shaping India's travel landscape. With a robust portfolio encompassing over 100+ properties nationwide and Neighbor Country like SirLanka & Nepal, we are committed to delivering unparalleled experiences to our guests. Established in 2001 by the visionary Mr. Chander K Baljee, our brand has evolved into a trusted name renowned for excellence and innovation in hospitality.

At Royal Orchid & Regenta Hotels, we cater to discerning business and leisure travelers seeking superior comfort, delectable cuisine, and genuine Indian hospitality. Our diverse range of modern accommodations, including hotels, resorts, long-stay suites, and inns, ensures that every guest finds their perfect sanctuary. Whether in bustling metro cities, serene holiday destinations, sacred pilgrimage sites, or exotic wildlife parks, our properties offer an oasis of experience and relaxation.

Headquartered in the Silicon Valley of India - Bengaluru, our dedicated team is passionate about hospitality, committed to delivering exceptional guest experiences at every touchpoint. Guided by our core values, we strive to create enduring value for our esteemed owners, delighted guests, dedicated staff, and esteemed shareholders.

Experience the essence of Indian hospitality with Royal Orchid & Regenta Hotels - where every stay is a memorable journey.
